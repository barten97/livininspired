We tend to live in quite a yang state, moving unconsciously from moment to moment with the days & minutes passing by without taking a moment to pause for integration and self care.

Yin is the state in which our nervous system has the opportunity to drop to the minimum, in order to regulate the most important functions that maintain sustenance for the longevity of life.

Yin Yoga is the long-held, passive nature of the posture, which gives time for a deeper journey to unfold, a journey into the meditative aspects of yoga, a bridge to living life mindfully.
