SOHA: School of Healing Arts 200hrs Yoga Teacher Training Modules

Ganesha  Ground: Earth Module 1

• Foundation of an integrated yoga practice.
• Discover the names, main function, and forms of grounding yoga poses.
•  Alignment principles and the healthy biomechanics of the practice.
• Principles of yoga philosophy applying the teachings into everyday life.
• Ganesha offerings: grounded, opening the obstacles and guiding the practice to solid
foundations.

Saraswati Sings: Water Module 2

 • We enter the realms of Saraswati. Creativity, Flow, and Krama (art of progressions) come into the game.
•  Learn teaching techniques and the dynamics of how to create an intelligent sequence.
 •  Study of the scriptures, and the self knowledge (Svadyaya)
•  Move like water, in and through our emotional bodies, healing, creating, and manifesting our
divine creativity.
• Embody the magic flow behind smart, safe, and progressive vinyasa sequencing.

Hanuman Leap:
 Air Module 4

Visit to Sound Healing/Kirtan Concert & Closing Ceremony
• Unfold the Love into the upper realms and Chakras.
• Learn Yin Yoga, the use of Props, and Restorative practice.
• Arise in your own style and inner teacher.
• Embodiment of an integrated and complete practice.
• Integrate Mantra, Mudra, Meditation, Krama, Cooling, and Closing.
• Yoga Sadhana becomes a complete piece of art.
• The Offering (Isvara Pranidhanani)
• Devotion to the Light Path.
• Closing Ceremony, Puja.
• Set your wings ready to fly. Take Flight and share it to the world.

Shiva Boom:
Fire Module 3

 • Fire Ceremony
• Embody Tapas: The Fire of Discipline
• Burn through impurities and leap into conquering fears
• Tune your teaching voice with wisdom and truth
• Re-define Teaching Methodology
• Dive deep into the art of assisting and adjusting.
• Feel complete confidence into mastering the art of touch with Acroyoga and Thai Massage.

ABOUT YOUR COURSE INSTRUCTORS

Carlos Romero

Founder of Livin' Inspired, The Chaman, Yin Yoga, Vinyasa Flow, Thai Masage, Sacred Sound Healing
Surfyogi devoted Yoga teacher, who has been immersed in the journey of self-exploration through Yoga for over the last 15 years. After finishing his first Yoga Teacher Training in Sivananda Divine Life Society School (2002) he has continued to deepen his Yoga practice and expand the scope of his studies in Asthanga, Vinyasa, Jivamukti, Dharma Mittra Yoga, Anusara, Yin and Embodied Flow. In 2005, he became a Thai Yoga Massage therapist and continued his exploration and studies into other forms of movement & healing. Discovered AcroYoga (2010) in a surf trip and since then has been dedicated to share this practice as a way to explore connection and confidence to all those who feel the beauty of combining Yoga, acrobatics & healing arts.
The Essence of Carlos’s teaching is to bring joy, inspiration and ancient wisdom from different arts into one, to experience yoga as a beautiful way to celebrate life and to embrace spirit which manifest within and all around.

Daniela Garza

Founder of SOHA: School of Healing Arts, Yoga TT, Co-founder and Spiritual Director of Sutra Yoga Monterrey. Double Degree in Monterrey and France 
SCertified in Osteopathy & Bioenergetics, Medical Magnet Therapy, Natural Medicine, Mandala Vinyasa, Prana Flow, Krama Yoga, Tantra Yoga, Acroyoga, and Thai Massage. World Traveler, Daniela has been immersed more than 15 years in bodywork and spiritual practices. Freedom, Wisdom, and Love gravitate around her shared practices. Devoted service, clear technique, and sensitive awareness are present through her teachings. Her work as a Yoga and Holistic practitioner inspires deep understanding and healing. She emphasizes in acquiring awareness and sensitivity on how to listen, feel, and support each individual energy back to the Self. Currently she offers workshops, retreats, bodywork, and trainings around the world including the sacred arts of Alternative Medicine, Healing Arts, Yoga, and Acroyoga.
