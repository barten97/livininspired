This training has been created as an invitation for yoga practitioners, bodyworkers, somatic therapists, dancers, couples or individuals who wants to go deeper into a mindful approach of touching, adjusting and working with others through the ancient teachings of Thai Yoga Massage and modern approaches to holistic healing focus on the Myofascial Release and integration process.

The Sacred Dance of Thai Yoga Massage is a transformative experience.
A deep revolution on the way we move, interact, communicate and especially the way we share touch as a form of healing. 

PROGRAM CONTENT & INCLUSIONS

 Morning Yoga & Embodied Movement Practices
• Somatic Meditations
• Principles of Touch
• Thai Yoga Massage Foundations
• Therapeutic Approaches: Spinal Elongation, Hips and Lower Back, Diaphragm, Neck and Shoulders
• Myofascial Release Techniques
• Thai Massage principles apply on adjusting yoga poses
• Somatic Awareness: Developing Quality of Touch & Body Reading
• 50hr Yoga Alliance Certification ( Contributes Towards 300hr Advanced Training or YACEP)
