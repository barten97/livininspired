A Basic Journey into the Art of Breathing
10 Hour Online Program

In Sanskrit prana is translated as vital life force, and the way we move prana, life force through our body is by consciously understanding and moving the breath in and throughout the chambers of our internal body.

PRANAYAMA EDITION

In this 10 hour immersion, we move through over 20 practices to support you in creating your foundation into the pathway of pranayama, to gain full understanding of these practices and the way the breath moves through our body, we will also be covering the anatomy of the breath, the core cylinder and meditations by the incredible Rachel Ellery. 

You will learn how to use these pranayama techniques as a door way to access and support the conscious, sustainable control of your daily attitudes and mind set, how and when to use them, the ancient philosophy and why and how they became so powerful. We will also understand the cleansing and natural purification effects on the body and mind that simply just the breath can portray.
